Do's and Don'ts for a HashLearn Now Tutor

A. Instant Lessons

HashLearn Now is a tutoring platform that helps students clear concepts and solve doubts around questions that they struggle with. Our goal is to help the student learn by providing the best on-demand, tutoring experience.
To achieve this goal and to maintain high standards of engagement on our platform, there are do's and don'ts to be followed as a tutor broadly defined under three key areas - Subject Knowledge, Communication, Speed and Accuracy.

1. Subject Knowledge

While we appreciate that you understand your chosen topics very well, it’s critical you are thorough with your concepts. Please revise beforehand and have material available for reference during sessions.
In the God-forbid-it-does-not-happen scenario that you are not familiar with a concept or question posed by the student, please do not try and offer a halfbaked answer in any situation. Any such sessions reported to us by students will result in the equivalent of a two-star rating and you will not be paid for the session.
Know your topics, do a good job, help others, earn money.

2. Communication

Do not be too casual with your language or words. Be professional and courteous. Take your session seriously. It can go a long way in ensuring your position as a much sought-after tutor on HashLearn Now.

3. Speed and accuracy

Practice makes perfect and can help improve your responsiveness in chat sessions. A very good grasp of subject knowledge, related tips and techniques is essential ensures you are on top of your game.

B. Standard chat responses for special questions you could receive.

Greeting: Hello < name > How may I help you today?

When you’re taking time to solve the question: Hey, I’ve seen your question and I’m working on it as fast as I can! Hey, I’m halfway through the problem. Just writing down the final steps.

End of session: I am glad you found this of help. Is there any other question you want help with from this topic? Thank you < name >. All the best!

General question about HashLearn Now (rates, billing issues, customer service, product etc.) : Thanks for your question. I'm afraid I only handle academic questions. You can direct that to now@hashlearn.com or use the Customer Support link in the sliding drawer.

When the student asks for your Phone Number/ Facebook ID/ email ID or asks for any other mode of communication: Sorry, I cannot share personal details on this platform.
